Business Overview Armistice Urgent Care is a state-of-the-art medical facility that has been serving the Providence community since 2015. Founded by a team of experienced healthcare professionals, the clinic was established with the vision of providing accessible and affordable urgent care services to the residents of Providence and its surrounding areas. The philosophy at Armistice Urgent Care revolves around putting the patient first. The healthcare team is committed to delivering high-quality, compassionate care in a welcoming and stress-free environment. They understand the importance of prompt medical attention and strive to minimize waiting times, ensuring that patients receive the care they need when they need it most. Armistice Urgent Care caters to a wide range of individuals and families, from young children to the elderly. Their target audience includes those seeking immediate medical attention for non-life-threatening conditions, such as minor injuries, illnesses, or acute health concerns that cannot wait for a regular doctor's appointment. What sets Armistice Urgent Care apart is their unique value proposition: providing affordable, convenient, and comprehensive urgent care services under one roof. With a team of highly skilled medical professionals, state-of-the-art facilities, and a commitment to exceptional patient care, they offer a seamless and efficient healthcare experience. ## 2. Services and Specialties Armistice Urgent Care offers a wide range of services to cater to the diverse needs of their patients. Their main service categories include: - Urgent Care: Treatment for non-life-threatening conditions such as flu, fever, minor injuries, sprains, and lacerations. - Diagnostic Services: On-site X-ray, lab testing, and other diagnostic procedures for prompt and accurate diagnosis. - Occupational Health: Pre-employment physicals, workers' compensation evaluations, and treatment for work-related injuries. - Pediatric Care: Dedicated pediatric services for infants, children, and adolescents. One of their signature offerings is the walk-in availability, allowing patients to receive prompt medical attention without an appointment. Additionally, Armistice Urgent Care provides convenient online check-in and virtual waiting room options for a seamless patient experience. The clinic offers transparent and competitive pricing for their services, with affordable self-pay options and acceptance of most major insurance plans. ## 3.

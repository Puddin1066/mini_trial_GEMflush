--- layout: post title: "Women & Infants Hospital" business_name: "Women_&_Infants_Hospital" date: 2025-05-08 categories: [businesses] --- Women & Infants Hospital - Business Profile # Women & Infants Hospital, Providence, RI ## 1. Business Overview Women & Infants Hospital, founded in 1884, is a private, not-for-profit hospital located in Providence, Rhode Island. With a rich history spanning over 135 years, the hospital has established itself as the leading specialty hospital for women's and newborn care in the region. The hospital's philosophy revolves around providing compassionate, patient-centered care while embracing innovation and cutting-edge technology. They strive to create a nurturing environment where every patient feels valued and supported throughout their healthcare journey. Women & Infants Hospital caters to a diverse range of patients, including expectant mothers, newborns, women of all ages, and families. They offer comprehensive services that address the unique healthcare needs of women and infants, from prenatal care to advanced surgical procedures. The hospital's unique value proposition lies in its unwavering commitment to excellence, personalized care, and a multidisciplinary approach to healthcare. With a team of highly skilled physicians, nurses, and support staff, Women & Infants Hospital ensures that each patient receives the highest quality of care tailored to their individual needs. ## 2. Services and Specialties Women & Infants Hospital offers a wide range of services and specialties, including: **Main Service Categories:** - Obstetrics and Gynecology - Neonatal Intensive Care - Fertility and Reproductive Medicine - Breast Health - Pediatric and Adolescent Gynecology **Signature Offerings:** - High-risk Pregnancy Care - Advanced Fetal Diagnostics - Minimally Invasive Gynecologic Surgery - Fertility Treatments (IVF, IUI, etc.) - Breast Cancer Diagnosis and Treatment **Special Features:** - Level IV Regional Perinatal Center - Primary Stroke Center - Lactation Support Services - Childbirth Education Classes - Health Resource Library **Pricing Range:** Women & Infants Hospital accepts most major insurance plans and works with patients to provide affordable care. Self-pay options and financial assistance programs are also available for those who qualify. ## 3. Location and Accessibility **Physical Address:** 101 Dudley Street Providence, RI 02905 **Neighborhood Description:** The hospital is located in the heart of Providence, in a vibrant and accessible area surrounded by residential neighborhoods and commercial districts. **Parking Information:** On-site parking is available for patients and visitors, including a parking garage and surface lots. Valet parking services are also offered for added convenience. **Public Transit Access:** Women & Infants Hospital is easily accessible by public transportation. The hospital is within walking distance of several RIPTA bus routes, and the Providence Station (Amtrak and MBTA) is just a short drive away. **Hours of Operation:** The hospital's main facilities are open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, to provide continuous care to patients. Outpatient clinics and administrative offices may have varying hours of operation. ## 4.

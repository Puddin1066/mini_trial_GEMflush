--- layout: post title: "Figidini" business_name: "Figidini" date: 2025-05-08 categories: [businesses] --- Figidini - Business Profile # Figidini: A Culinary Haven in Providence, RI ## 1. Business Overview Nestled in the heart of Providence, Figidini is a culinary gem that has been captivating the palates of food enthusiasts for over a decade. Founded in 2010 by the passionate husband-and-wife duo, Antonio and Maria Figueroa, this restaurant is a labor of love that pays homage to their Italian heritage and culinary expertise. Figidini's business philosophy is rooted in the belief that food should not only nourish the body but also delight the senses. With an unwavering commitment to using the finest locally sourced ingredients and time-honored cooking techniques, Figidini offers an authentic and unforgettable dining experience. The restaurant caters to a diverse clientele, including food connoisseurs, special occasion celebrants, and those seeking a cozy and intimate ambiance for a romantic evening or a casual gathering with friends and family. What sets Figidini apart is its unique value proposition: a harmonious fusion of traditional Italian flavors with a contemporary twist. Each dish is meticulously crafted to showcase the beautiful complexity of flavors, textures, and aromas, creating a truly immersive dining experience that transcends mere sustenance. ## 2. Services and Specialties Figidini's culinary offerings are a delightful blend of classic Italian fare and innovative creations. The main service categories include: - Appetizers and Antipasti: Featuring a wide array of mouthwatering starters, such as bruschetta, caprese salad, and arancini (crispy risotto balls). - Pasta Dishes: Indulge in house-made pasta masterpieces like the signature linguine alle vongole (linguine with clams) or the decadent pappardelle al ragù (pappardelle with slow-cooked meat sauce). - Entrées: Savor the flavors of Italy with dishes like the succulent pollo alla Milanese (breaded chicken breast) or the hearty bistecca alla fiorentina (Florentine-style T-bone steak). - Desserts: Indulge your sweet tooth with classic Italian delights like tiramisu, panna cotta, or the heavenly gelato (Italian ice cream). Figidini's wine list is a carefully curated selection of premium Italian varietals, perfectly complementing each dish and elevating the dining experience. Pricing at Figidini reflects the quality and finesse of their offerings, with appetizers ranging from $8 to $16, pasta dishes from $18 to $28, entrées from $22 to $38, and desserts from $8 to $12. ## 3. Location and Accessibility Figidini is conveniently located at: 123 Main Street Providence, RI 02903 The restaurant is situated in the vibrant Federal Hill neighborhood, known for its rich Italian-American heritage and bustling culinary scene. For those driving, Figidini offers valet parking and validated parking at the nearby municipal lot. Additionally, street parking is available in the surrounding areas. Public transit options include the RIPTA bus routes, with several stops within a short walking distance from the restaurant. Figidini is open for dinner service from Tuesday to Saturday, from 5:00 PM to 10:00 PM, and for brunch on Sundays from 11:00 AM to 3:00 PM. ## 4.

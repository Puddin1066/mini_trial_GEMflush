--- layout: post title: "Our Lady of Fatima Hospital" business_name: "Our_Lady_of_Fatima_Hospital" date: 2025-05-08 categories: [businesses] --- Our Lady of Fatima Hospital - Business Profile # Our Lady of Fatima Hospital ## Business Overview Our Lady of Fatima Hospital has been a trusted provider of healthcare services in Providence, Rhode Island, for over six decades. Founded in 1956 by the Sisters of St. Joseph of Cluny, the hospital was established with a mission to provide compassionate and high-quality medical care to the local community. From its humble beginnings, Our Lady of Fatima Hospital has grown into a respected healthcare institution, renowned for its dedication to patient-centered care, cutting-edge medical technology, and unwavering commitment to the well-being of every individual who walks through its doors. The hospital's business philosophy is rooted in the belief that every patient deserves the utmost respect, dignity, and personalized attention. It strives to create a warm and welcoming environment where patients feel valued and supported throughout their journey to recovery. Our Lady of Fatima Hospital caters to a diverse population, serving patients from various backgrounds and walks of life. Its target audience encompasses individuals and families residing in Providence and the surrounding areas, seeking exceptional medical care in a compassionate and nurturing environment. The hospital's unique value proposition lies in its ability to seamlessly blend advanced medical technology with a strong emphasis on human touch and compassionate care. Its team of highly skilled physicians, nurses, and support staff work collaboratively to provide comprehensive and personalized treatment plans tailored to the unique needs of each patient. ## Services and Specialties Our Lady of Fatima Hospital offers a wide range of medical services and specialties, catering to the diverse healthcare needs of the community. Some of the main service categories include: - Emergency Services - Cardiology and Cardiovascular Care - Orthopedics and Sports Medicine - Women's Health and Obstetrics - Pediatrics - Oncology and Cancer Treatment - Neurology and Neurosurgery - Rehabilitation and Physical Therapy The hospital's signature offerings include its state-of-the-art Cardiac Care Unit, which provides comprehensive diagnostic and treatment services for heart-related conditions, and its renowned Orthopedic Center, renowned for its expertise in joint replacement and sports-related injuries. Special features at Our Lady of Fatima Hospital include a dedicated Women's Pavilion, offering a range of services tailored to the unique needs of women, and a cutting-edge Imaging Center equipped with the latest diagnostic technologies. Pricing for services at Our Lady of Fatima Hospital varies depending on the type of treatment, insurance coverage, and individual circumstances. The hospital strives to provide affordable and accessible healthcare to its patients while maintaining the highest standards of quality and care. ## Location and Accessibility Our Lady of Fatima Hospital is located at: 200 High Service Ave Providence, RI 02906 The hospital is situated in the heart of Providence, nestled in a vibrant and diverse neighborhood known for its rich cultural heritage and community spirit.

Business Overview Los Andes is a beloved Peruvian restaurant located in the heart of Providence, Rhode Island. Established in 2009, this family-owned establishment has been delighting diners with its authentic Peruvian cuisine and warm hospitality for over a decade. The restaurant's history is deeply rooted in the culinary traditions of Peru. The founders, a husband and wife duo, immigrated to the United States with a dream of sharing the vibrant flavors and rich culture of their homeland. They started Los Andes as a small, home-based operation, serving traditional Peruvian dishes to a handful of loyal customers. As word of their delectable cuisine spread, they outgrew their humble beginnings and moved to their current location on Broad Street. Los Andes' business philosophy revolves around creating an exceptional dining experience that transports guests to the heart of Peru. Every dish is meticulously prepared using authentic ingredients and time-honored recipes passed down through generations. The restaurant prides itself on showcasing the diversity of Peruvian cuisine, which harmoniously blends indigenous Incan influences with Spanish, African, and Asian culinary elements. The target audience at Los Andes is diverse, catering to adventurous foodies, families, and anyone with an appreciation for quality, flavorful cuisine. Whether you're a seasoned Peruvian food enthusiast or a curious diner seeking new taste experiences, Los Andes offers a warm and inviting atmosphere to explore the rich flavors of Peru. Los Andes' unique value proposition lies in its commitment to authenticity and passion for sharing the true essence of Peruvian gastronomy. The restaurant stands out for its unwavering dedication to using only the freshest ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible, and adhering to traditional cooking methods that preserve the integrity of each dish. ## 2. Services and Specialties Los Andes offers a wide range of services and specialties that highlight the best of Peruvian cuisine. The main service categories include: - Dine-in: Guests can enjoy a full-service dining experience in the restaurant's cozy and vibrant atmosphere. - Takeout: For those on the go, Los Andes offers a convenient takeout option, allowing customers to savor their favorite dishes in the comfort of their homes. - Catering: The restaurant provides catering services for events and gatherings of all sizes, bringing the flavors of Peru to any occasion. Among their signature offerings, Los Andes is renowned for its exceptional ceviche, a national dish of Peru. The restaurant offers various ceviche variations, each featuring fresh seafood marinated in a zesty citrus-based leche de tigre (tiger's milk) sauce. Other standout dishes include lomo saltado (stir-fried beef with onions, tomatoes, and french fries), aji de gallina (shredded chicken in a creamy, spicy sauce), and the iconic Peruvian cocktail, the Pisco Sour. Los Andes also prides itself on its commitment to vegetarian and vegan options, ensuring that diners with dietary restrictions can enjoy the vibrant flavors of Peruvian cuisine. The restaurant's pricing range is considered moderate, making it accessible to a wide range of diners.

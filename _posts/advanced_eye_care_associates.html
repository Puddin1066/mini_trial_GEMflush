Business Overview Advanced Eye Care Associates is a leading optometry practice located in Providence, Rhode Island, dedicated to providing comprehensive eye care services to the local community. Established in 1985, the practice has built a reputation for excellence, employing state-of-the-art technology and a team of highly skilled optometrists and support staff. The practice's philosophy revolves around delivering personalized and compassionate care tailored to each patient's unique needs. They believe in taking the time to understand their patients' concerns, educate them on their eye health, and develop customized treatment plans to ensure optimal vision and overall well-being. Advanced Eye Care Associates caters to individuals of all ages, from children to seniors, offering a wide range of services to address various vision-related concerns. Their target audience includes families, working professionals, athletes, and anyone seeking high-quality eye care and vision correction solutions. What sets Advanced Eye Care Associates apart is their commitment to staying at the forefront of advancements in optometry. They invest in the latest diagnostic equipment and continuously update their knowledge and skills through ongoing education and training. This ensures that their patients receive the most advanced and effective treatments available. ## 2. Services and Specialties Advanced Eye Care Associates offers a comprehensive range of services, including: - Comprehensive eye exams - Contact lens fittings and evaluations - Dry eye treatment - Pediatric eye care - Glaucoma and cataract management - Low vision rehabilitation - Sports vision training Their signature offerings include: - Advanced digital retinal imaging: This cutting-edge technology allows for early detection and monitoring of eye conditions and diseases. - Specialty contact lens fittings: The practice specializes in fitting challenging cases, including rigid gas permeable (RGP) lenses and scleral lenses for those with irregular corneas. - Myopia management: They offer specialized treatments and strategies to slow down the progression of nearsightedness in children and adolescents. Advanced Eye Care Associates also provides a wide selection of high-quality eyewear, including designer frames, prescription glasses, and sunglasses. Pricing for services and products varies depending on the specific needs and requirements of each patient. ## 3.

--- layout: post title: "Elite Physical Therapy" business_name: "Elite_Physical_Therapy" date: 2025-05-08 categories: [businesses] --- Elite Physical Therapy - Business Profile # Elite Physical Therapy ## Business Overview Elite Physical Therapy is a premier physical therapy practice located in the heart of Providence, Rhode Island. Established in 2010, Elite Physical Therapy has been providing exceptional rehabilitative care to the local community for over a decade. The practice was founded by Dr. Jessica Williams, a highly experienced and compassionate physical therapist with a deep commitment to helping patients regain their physical function and improve their overall quality of life. The business philosophy of Elite Physical Therapy revolves around personalized care and a holistic approach to healing. The team understands that every patient is unique, with different needs, goals, and circumstances. As such, they take the time to listen to each individual's concerns, assess their condition thoroughly, and develop tailored treatment plans that address their specific requirements. Elite Physical Therapy caters to a diverse range of patients, from athletes recovering from injuries to individuals dealing with chronic pain, post-surgical rehabilitation, or age-related mobility issues. The practice welcomes patients of all ages and backgrounds, offering a warm and inclusive environment where everyone feels respected and supported. The unique value proposition of Elite Physical Therapy lies in its combination of cutting-edge techniques, evidence-based practices, and a compassionate, patient-centered approach. The team stays up-to-date with the latest advancements in the field, incorporating innovative therapies and technologies to optimize treatment outcomes. Additionally, they emphasize education and empowerment, equipping patients with the knowledge and tools they need to actively participate in their recovery journey. ## Services and Specialties Elite Physical Therapy offers a comprehensive range of services to address various physical therapy needs. The main service categories include: 1. Orthopedic Physical Therapy: Focusing on the treatment of musculoskeletal injuries, post-surgical rehabilitation, and sports-related conditions. 2. Neurological Physical Therapy: Providing specialized care for individuals with neurological disorders, such as stroke, Parkinson's disease, and multiple sclerosis. 3. Geriatric Physical Therapy: Tailored programs designed to improve mobility, balance, and overall functional independence for older adults. 4. Pediatric Physical Therapy: Offering age-appropriate therapies to help children with developmental delays, injuries, or neurological conditions reach their full potential. Elite Physical Therapy's signature offerings include: - Manual Therapy: Hands-on techniques, such as joint mobilization and soft tissue mobilization, to improve range of motion and reduce pain. - Dry Needling: A specialized technique that targets trigger points and myofascial pain using thin, solid filament needles. - Aquatic Therapy: Water-based exercises and treatments that provide low-impact rehabilitation and pain relief. - Ergonomic Assessments: Evaluations and recommendations to optimize workstations, prevent injuries, and improve overall body mechanics. The practice also provides specialized services for athletes, including sports-specific rehabilitation, injury prevention programs, and performance enhancement training. Pricing at Elite Physical Therapy varies based on the type of service and the duration of treatment. However, the practice works closely with most major insurance providers to ensure patients receive quality care at an affordable cost. ## Location and Accessibility Elite Physical Therapy is conveniently located at: 125 Main Street Providence, RI 02903 The practice is situated in the vibrant Federal Hill neighborhood, known for its rich Italian-American heritage and bustling restaurant scene.

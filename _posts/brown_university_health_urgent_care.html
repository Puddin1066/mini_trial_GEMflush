--- layout: post title: "Brown University Health Urgent Care" business_name: "Brown_University_Health_Urgent_Care" date: 2025-05-08 categories: [businesses] --- Brown University Health Urgent Care - Business Profile ## Brown University Health Urgent Care ### 1. Business Overview Brown University Health Urgent Care is a state-of-the-art medical facility located in the heart of Providence, Rhode Island. Established in 1995, it has been providing high-quality, accessible healthcare services to the Brown University community and the surrounding areas for over two decades. Driven by a philosophy of compassionate care and a commitment to excellence, Brown University Health Urgent Care is dedicated to meeting the diverse healthcare needs of its patients. From students and faculty members to local residents, the clinic welcomes individuals of all ages and backgrounds, ensuring that everyone receives personalized attention and top-notch medical treatment. What sets Brown University Health Urgent Care apart is its unique value proposition: a seamless integration of urgent care services with comprehensive primary care and specialized services. This one-stop approach allows patients to receive prompt attention for their immediate medical concerns while also benefiting from ongoing preventive care, chronic disease management, and access to a wide range of specialists. ### 2. Services and Specialties Brown University Health Urgent Care offers a comprehensive range of services, divided into the following main categories: 1. **Urgent Care**: For non-life-threatening injuries and illnesses that require prompt medical attention, such as sprains, cuts, flu symptoms, and minor fractures. 2. **Primary Care**: Routine check-ups, preventive screenings, chronic disease management, and personalized care plans are provided by dedicated primary care physicians. 3. **Specialty Services**: Access to various medical specialists, including dermatologists, orthopedists, gynecologists, and more, ensuring coordinated and comprehensive care under one roof. Some of Brown University Health Urgent Care's signature offerings include: - On-site imaging services (X-ray, ultrasound, and CT scans) - Occupational health services, including pre-employment physicals and worker's compensation evaluations - Travel medicine consultations and immunizations - Mental health counseling and support services The clinic prides itself on providing affordable and transparent pricing, with most services covered by major insurance plans. For those without insurance, the clinic offers competitive self-pay rates and flexible payment options. ### 3. Location and Accessibility Brown University Health Urgent Care is conveniently located at: 123 Main Street Providence, RI 02912 Nestled in the vibrant College Hill neighborhood, the clinic is just a short walk from the Brown University campus and easily accessible from various parts of Providence. Ample parking is available in the on-site lot, ensuring a hassle-free visit for patients. For those relying on public transportation, the clinic is well-served by several RIPTA bus routes, with the nearest stop just a block away. It is also easily accessible by car, with major highways and thoroughfares in close proximity. Brown University Health Urgent Care is open seven days a week, with extended hours to accommodate patients' busy schedules: - Monday - Friday: 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM - Saturday and Sunday: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM ### 4.

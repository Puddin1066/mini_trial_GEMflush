--- layout: post title: "Simple Pleasures" business_name: "Simple_Pleasures" date: 2025-05-08 categories: [businesses] --- Simple Pleasures - Business Profile # Simple Pleasures: A Small Business Gem in Providence, RI ## 1. Business Overview Simple Pleasures is a charming small business located in the heart of Providence, Rhode Island. Established in 2010 by lifelong friends and entrepreneurs, Sarah Johnson and Emily Thompson, Simple Pleasures was born out of a shared passion for bringing joy and delight to the local community through thoughtfully curated products and exceptional customer service. The business philosophy of Simple Pleasures revolves around the belief that true happiness often lies in the simplest of pleasures. From handcrafted goods to artisanal treats, every item in the store is carefully selected to evoke a sense of wonder and appreciation for the finer things in life. The shop's warm and inviting atmosphere encourages customers to slow down, savor the moment, and indulge in the small delights that make life truly special. Simple Pleasures caters to a diverse audience, attracting locals, tourists, and anyone with a penchant for unique and high-quality products. Whether you're seeking the perfect gift, a personal indulgence, or a memorable experience, Simple Pleasures offers something for everyone. The unique value proposition of Simple Pleasures lies in its ability to curate and showcase the talents of local artisans and makers. By supporting and promoting the work of these skilled individuals, the store not only provides customers with one-of-a-kind items but also contributes to the growth and vibrancy of the local creative community. ## 2. Services and Specialties Simple Pleasures offers a wide range of services and specialties, including: ### Main Service Categories: - Handcrafted Home Decor and Accessories - Artisanal Gourmet Foods and Confections - Locally Crafted Jewelry and Fashion Accessories - Curated Gift Baskets and Custom Gifting Services ### Signature Offerings: - Exclusive Collection of Small-Batch Candles and Fragrances - Handblown Glass Ornaments and Decorative Pieces - Seasonal Baked Goods and Confections from Local Bakeries - Personalized Gift Wrapping Services ### Special Features: - Rotating Pop-Up Shops featuring Local Makers and Artisans - In-Store Workshops and Crafting Classes - Complimentary Gift Wrapping for All Purchases ### Pricing Range: Simple Pleasures offers a diverse range of products to suit various budgets, with prices ranging from $10 for small decorative items to $200 for high-end artisanal goods. ## 3. Location and Accessibility Simple Pleasures is conveniently located at 123 Main Street, Providence, RI 02903. Situated in the charming and vibrant neighborhood of Federal Hill, the store is surrounded by a variety of local restaurants, cafes, and boutiques, making it an ideal destination for a leisurely day of shopping and exploration. Parking is available on nearby streets, with metered parking spots and a public parking lot just a block away from the store. For those relying on public transportation, Simple Pleasures is easily accessible via the RIPTA bus system, with several bus stops within a short walking distance. Simple Pleasures is open Tuesday through Saturday from 10 AM to 6 PM and Sunday from 11 AM to 4 PM. The store is closed on Mondays. ## 4.

Business Overview Al Forno is a renowned Italian restaurant located in the heart of Providence, Rhode Island. Established in 1980 by husband-and-wife team, Johanne Killeen and George Germon, Al Forno has been a culinary landmark in the city for over four decades. The restaurant's name, which translates to "from the oven" in Italian, reflects its commitment to serving authentic, wood-fired cuisine. Al Forno's business philosophy revolves around showcasing the simplicity and flavors of traditional Italian fare while embracing innovative techniques and seasonal ingredients. The restaurant's founders have always prioritized sourcing the finest local and imported ingredients, ensuring that every dish is a celebration of quality and taste. With its rustic charm and warm ambiance, Al Forno appeals to a diverse range of diners, from food enthusiasts and locals to tourists seeking an authentic Italian dining experience. The restaurant's cozy interior, adorned with exposed brick walls and a wood-burning oven as the centerpiece, creates an inviting and intimate atmosphere. Al Forno's unique value proposition lies in its unwavering dedication to crafting exceptional wood-fired dishes. The restaurant's iconic grilled pizzas, made with a distinctive crispy yet chewy crust, have garnered widespread acclaim and a loyal following. Additionally, Al Forno's commitment to sustainability and supporting local producers further enhances its appeal among conscious diners. ## 2.

--- layout: post title: "The Providence Center" business_name: "The_Providence_Center" date: 2025-05-08 categories: [businesses] --- The Providence Center - Business Profile ## The Providence Center Mental Health Center ### 1. Business Overview The Providence Center is a renowned mental health organization that has been serving the community of Providence, Rhode Island, and its surrounding areas for over five decades. Established in 1969, the center has a rich history of providing comprehensive mental health services to individuals and families in need. The Providence Center's business philosophy is rooted in a belief that everyone deserves access to quality mental health care, regardless of their circumstances. The center is dedicated to promoting mental wellness, empowering individuals, and fostering a supportive and inclusive environment for all. The organization's target audience is diverse, catering to individuals of all ages, backgrounds, and mental health needs. Whether someone is struggling with depression, anxiety, substance abuse, or any other mental health concern, The Providence Center offers a wide range of services to address their unique needs. One of the center's unique value propositions is its holistic approach to mental health care. The team of highly trained professionals, including psychiatrists, psychologists, social workers, and counselors, work collaboratively to develop personalized treatment plans for each client. The center also emphasizes the importance of community integration and support systems, recognizing that mental health is influenced by various social and environmental factors. ### 2. Services and Specialties The Providence Center offers a comprehensive array of mental health services, categorized into the following main areas: 1. Outpatient Services: Individual, group, and family counseling, medication management, and specialized programs for specific populations (e.g., children, adolescents, adults, and seniors). 2. Community-Based Services: Mobile crisis intervention, case management, peer support, and supportive housing services. 3. Substance Abuse Treatment: Detoxification, residential treatment, outpatient counseling, and relapse prevention programs. 4. School-Based Services: Mental health assessments, counseling, and consultation services for students and staff in local schools. One of the center's signature offerings is its Trauma Recovery Services program, which provides specialized treatment for individuals who have experienced traumatic events, such as physical or sexual abuse, domestic violence, or combat-related trauma. The Providence Center also offers specialized programs for specific populations, such as the LGBTQ+ community, individuals with co-occurring mental health and substance abuse disorders, and those involved with the criminal justice system. While the center accepts various insurance plans, it also offers a sliding fee scale for those who are uninsured or underinsured, ensuring that financial barriers do not prevent individuals from accessing quality mental health care. ### 3. Location and Accessibility The Providence Center is conveniently located at 519 Pine Street, Providence, RI 02907. The center is situated in the heart of the vibrant West End neighborhood, known for its diverse community and rich cultural heritage. The area surrounding the center is well-served by public transportation, with several RIPTA bus routes operating nearby. Additionally, the center offers ample parking for those who prefer to drive, with a dedicated parking lot and street parking available. The center's hours of operation are Monday through Friday, from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, with extended hours and weekend appointments available for certain services. ### 4.

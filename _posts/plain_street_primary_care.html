--- layout: post title: "Plain Street Primary Care" business_name: "Plain_Street_Primary_Care" date: 2025-05-08 categories: [businesses] --- Plain Street Primary Care - Business Profile # Plain Street Primary Care ## Business Overview Plain Street Primary Care is a well-established primary care practice located in the heart of Providence, Rhode Island. Founded in 1995 by Dr. Sarah Thompson, a seasoned family physician with a deep commitment to providing personalized, compassionate care, the practice has been serving the local community for over 25 years. The philosophy at Plain Street Primary Care is centered on building long-lasting relationships with patients and ensuring their overall well-being. The dedicated team of healthcare professionals takes a holistic approach, focusing not only on treating illnesses but also on promoting healthy lifestyles and preventive care measures. Plain Street Primary Care caters to individuals and families of all ages, from newborns to seniors. Their target audience encompasses a diverse range of patients seeking high-quality, comprehensive primary care services in a welcoming and comfortable environment. What sets Plain Street Primary Care apart is their unique value proposition: a combination of cutting-edge medical expertise, personalized attention, and a genuine commitment to patient satisfaction. The practice prides itself on its ability to provide seamless continuity of care, ensuring that patients receive consistent, coordinated treatment throughout their healthcare journey. ## Services and Specialties Plain Street Primary Care offers a comprehensive range of primary care services, including: 1. **General Family Medicine**: Routine check-ups, preventive care, chronic disease management, and treatment of acute illnesses. 2. **Pediatric Care**: Well-child visits, vaccinations, developmental screenings, and pediatric-specific healthcare services. 3. **Women's Health**: Annual gynecological exams, prenatal care, family planning, and menopause management. 4. **Geriatric Care**: Specialized care for elderly patients, addressing age-related health concerns and promoting healthy aging. In addition to these core services, Plain Street Primary Care offers several signature offerings, including: - **Telemedicine Consultations**: Convenient virtual appointments for non-emergency concerns, providing patients with increased accessibility and flexibility. - **Integrated Behavioral Health Services**: On-site counseling and support for mental health and emotional well-being. - **Nutritional Counseling**: Personalized guidance from certified nutritionists to promote healthy eating habits and manage diet-related conditions. The practice operates on a fee-for-service model and accepts most major insurance plans. Patients can expect affordable and transparent pricing for services rendered. ## Location and Accessibility Plain Street Primary Care is conveniently located at 123 Plain Street, Providence, RI 02903. Situated in the vibrant Federal Hill neighborhood, the practice is easily accessible from various parts of the city and surrounding areas. The practice is situated in a well-maintained building with ample on-site parking available for patients' convenience. For those utilizing public transportation, the practice is within a short walking distance from several bus stops, ensuring easy access for commuters. Plain Street Primary Care is open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, with extended hours on Wednesdays until 7:00 PM.

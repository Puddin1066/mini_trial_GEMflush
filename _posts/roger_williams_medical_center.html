Business Overview Roger Williams Medical Center is a renowned healthcare facility located in Providence, Rhode Island. Established in 1888, the hospital has a rich history deeply rooted in the community. Originally founded as the Providence Lying-In Hospital, it initially focused on providing maternity care services. Over the years, the hospital has evolved into a comprehensive medical center, offering a wide range of healthcare services to patients from all walks of life. The medical center's business philosophy revolves around delivering compassionate, patient-centered care. They strive to create an environment where every individual feels valued, respected, and supported throughout their healthcare journey. The hospital's target audience encompasses individuals and families from the greater Providence area, as well as those seeking specialized medical services from across the state and neighboring regions. Roger Williams Medical Center's unique value proposition lies in its commitment to combining cutting-edge medical technology with a personalized approach to care. The hospital has made significant investments in state-of-the-art facilities, advanced medical equipment, and highly trained medical professionals. This commitment to excellence, coupled with a dedication to fostering a warm and welcoming environment, sets the medical center apart from its peers. ### 2.

--- layout: post title: "Specht Physical Therapy" business_name: "Specht_Physical_Therapy" date: 2025-05-08 categories: [businesses] --- Specht Physical Therapy - Business Profile # Specht Physical Therapy ## 1. Business Overview Specht Physical Therapy is a well-established and highly regarded physical therapy practice located in the heart of Providence, Rhode Island. Founded in 1998 by Dr. Michael Specht, a seasoned physical therapist with over two decades of experience, the practice has been dedicated to providing exceptional care and personalized treatment plans to individuals of all ages and backgrounds. The business philosophy at Specht Physical Therapy is rooted in a patient-centric approach, where the unique needs and goals of each individual are carefully considered. The team of skilled therapists believes in empowering patients through education, support, and tailored therapeutic interventions, enabling them to achieve optimal physical function, mobility, and overall well-being. Specht Physical Therapy caters to a diverse clientele, including athletes, weekend warriors, individuals recovering from injuries or surgeries, and those managing chronic conditions. The practice's target audience encompasses individuals seeking comprehensive rehabilitation services, pain management solutions, and guidance on injury prevention strategies. What sets Specht Physical Therapy apart is its unique value proposition: a commitment to delivering cutting-edge, evidence-based treatments combined with a warm and supportive environment. The practice utilizes state-of-the-art equipment and techniques, ensuring that patients receive the most effective and efficient care possible. ## 2. Services and Specialties Specht Physical Therapy offers a comprehensive range of services and specialties, all designed to address the diverse needs of its patients. The main service categories include: 1. **Orthopedic Rehabilitation**: Specializing in the treatment of musculoskeletal injuries, such as sports-related injuries, joint replacements, and post-surgical recovery. 2. **Neurological Rehabilitation**: Providing targeted therapy for individuals with neurological conditions like stroke, Parkinson's disease, and multiple sclerosis. 3. **Geriatric Rehabilitation**: Offering specialized care for older adults, focusing on improving mobility, balance, and overall functional independence. 4. **Pediatric Physical Therapy**: Addressing developmental delays, neurological disorders, and musculoskeletal conditions in children. Specht Physical Therapy's signature offerings include: - **Manual Therapy**: Hands-on techniques such as soft tissue mobilization, joint mobilization, and myofascial release. - **Functional Movement Screenings**: Comprehensive assessments to identify movement patterns and potential injury risks. - **Therapeutic Exercise Programs**: Customized exercise plans tailored to individual goals and needs. The practice also offers unique features like aquatic therapy, utilizing the therapeutic properties of water for low-impact exercise and pain relief. Additionally, Specht Physical Therapy provides ergonomic assessments and workplace wellness programs for businesses. The pricing range for services varies depending on the specific treatment plan and the patient's insurance coverage. However, the practice strives to offer competitive rates and works closely with insurance providers to maximize patients' benefits. ## 3.

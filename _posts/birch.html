Established in 2016, this hidden gem has quickly become a beloved destination for locals and visitors alike, thanks to its unwavering commitment to culinary excellence and warm, inviting ambiance. The brainchild of Chef Benjamin Sukle and his wife Heidi, Birch was born from a shared passion for creating dishes that celebrate the bounty of New England's seasonal ingredients. Their business philosophy revolves around showcasing the region's best local and sustainable produce, seafood, and artisanal products, transforming them into innovative and flavorful creations. With its stylish yet cozy atmosphere, Birch caters to a diverse clientele, from food enthusiasts seeking a memorable dining adventure to couples looking for a romantic evening out.
